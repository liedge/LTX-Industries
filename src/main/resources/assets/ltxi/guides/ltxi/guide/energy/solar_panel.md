# Solar Panel

<BlockImage id="solar_panel" scale="4" />

Solar Panels are passive power generators. It has a very low energy generation rate and use is restricted to overworld-type
dimensions during the daytime. But energy is free and requires no jump-starting, so they are the most reliable fallback
power sources for critical infrastructure like your digital item storage or your main power plant.

> Solar Panels will work in the rain, but not thunderstorms.
