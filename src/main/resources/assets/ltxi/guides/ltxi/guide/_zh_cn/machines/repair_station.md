# Repair Station

<BlockImage id="repair_station" scale="4" />

We understand that not all equipment can use our state of the art ε or Wonderland technology, and work off of the inferior
durability system. The Repair Station exists to cover such cases. Using <Color id="energy">Energy</Color>, this machine
will fuse a piece of equipment's molecular structure back to its original state indefinitely. Provided that you do not
break the tool before you are able to service it, of course.

> Items tagged `#ltxi:repair_blacklist` cannot be repaired, nor any other item that uses non-standard durability mechanics.
